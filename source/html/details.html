A neighbourhood place and now a Timaru institution, TGB (The Grosvenor Bar) is located in a one of a kind historical space with dramatic high ceilings, nostalgic exposed brick and modern touches. Breakfast is served in the restaurant and guest selections are made from a tempting menu on check-in.
