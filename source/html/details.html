The Grosvenor Hotel

View website 
Our hotel rooms and common area’s feature art by local and international artists. The hotel features 33 rooms and all our rooms have an ensuite with hairdryer, ironing board & iron, tea & coffee making facilities, complimentary Wi-Fi and TV. 

A neighbourhood place and now a Timaru institution, TGB (The Grosvenor Bar) is located in a one of a kind historical space with dramatic high ceilings, nostalgic exposed brick and modern touches. Breakfast is served in the restaurant and guest selections are made from a tempting menu on check-in. Dinner in our restaurant is highly recommended; a convivial atmosphere where locals and guests enjoy authentic Southern hospitality.
