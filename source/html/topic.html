Timaru and surrounds

Flanked by the majestic snowed-capped Southern Alps in the west and bordered by the fresh clean waters of the Rangitata and Pareroa rivers in the north and south, Timaru is a picturesque rural town with a big country heart.

Canterbury’s second largest city, it's a great place for a family getaway and for backbackers on the move. It's the ideal gateway to the beautiful MacKenzie district. And it's the perfectly-placed midpoint for travellers on the hop between Christchurch and Dunedin.
